Anyone can call themselves a conspiracy theorist. Theorists sometimes have a
difficult time because other people insult and make assumptions about them.
One of the greatest platforms used by theorists is social media, more specifically Youtube.
A quick search will result in hundreds of different people wanting to give their opinion on a theory.
Depending on the person and the event, a theory can be full of facts and some type pf proof.

Shane Dawson:

Shane Dawson is an online influencer who produces videos on the social media platform known as youtube.
He has over 18 million subscribers and produces lots of content that gets over a million views in a few days. His content ranges from skits to food reviews,
product reviews, and conspiracy theories. He is now known as the "Oprah" of Youtube for his popular series
on beauty guru Jeffree Star and currently on Jake Paul where he tries to go deep into their lives and success.


Kendall Rae:

Kendall Rae is another Youtube influencer and has over 950,000 subscribers on her youtube channel.
Her channel has evolved from lifestyle and makeup tutortials to solely on conspiracy theories and the occasional
lifestyle videos. She has collaborated briefly before with Shane Dawson as well as her husband Josh Thomas. Something that she frequently
does on her channel is advertise merchandise where all of the profits go to organizations.

The Buzzfeed Unsolved Network:

The Buzzfeed Unsolved Network crew is a Youtube Channel that is dedicated to bringing spooky videos to it's audience.
Their most recognized hosts include Shane Madej (left) and Ryan Bergara (right). The channel used to be part of Buzzfeed's other channel "Buzzfeed blue" which consisted of many types of videos including the
occassional mystery videos from Buzzfeed Unsolved. Since creating their own separate channel, there are more contributions from other
Buzzfeed creators as well as true crime and supernatural content from Ryan and Shane.

Josh Thomas

Josh Thomas is a Youtube influencer who has over 93,000 subscribers. His content is mainly focused on mysteries that
have to do with space and science. He has collaborated with his wife Kendall Rae and has even started a podcast together with her
called the "Mile Higher podcast."
